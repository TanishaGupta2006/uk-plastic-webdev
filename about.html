About UK Plastic Industries

UK Plastic Industries is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality dinnerware, with a legacy that began in 1978. Founded by the late Mrs. Kailash Rani Aggarwal, the company has grown steadily over the decades, guided by strong values, continuous innovation, and an uncompromising commitment to quality. With extensive industry experience, we have consistently evolved our product range, designs, sizes, and manufacturing capabilities to keep pace with changing market needs. Our focus on durability, functionality, and modern aesthetics has helped us build long-standing relationships with clients across the hospitality and retail sectors.

Our mission is simple to deliver durable, thoughtfully designed, and reliable tableware that meets the highest standards of performance and finish. Every product is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring it is practical for everyday use while maintaining visual appeal.

We take pride in offering a wide range of designs at competitive prices, supported by timely delivery and responsive customer service. Whether it’s a general inquiry or a customized requirement, our team is always ready to provide dependable support and solutions.
