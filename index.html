Welcome to UK Plastic Industries

It has been active in the local industry for several decades, building a reputation for offering a wide range of acrylic, melamine, and tableware products that cater to both retail customers and commercial clients such as restaurants, hotels, and catering services The company’s product portfolio includes acrylic plates, serving trays, bowls, serving sets, and complete dinner sets, available in multiple colors, shapes, and sizes to suit diverse requirements. These products are lightweight, durable, hygienic, and easy to maintain making them ideal for everyday use as well as hospitality environments..

UK Plastic Industries is a distinguished manufacturing brand based in Pathankot, Punjab. Established in 1978 by Late Mrs. Kailash Rani Aggarwal, the company has built a legacy of excellence in crafting premium acrylic, melamine, and bone china tableware along with customised solutions. Rooted in quality, precision, and timeless design, UK Plastic Industries serves retailers, wholesalers, and hospitality businesses with long-lasting products, elegant finishes, and dependable service upholding a standard trusted for generations.
